# 🚀 30 Days of Agentic AI: From Fundamentals to Production

Welcome to my **30-Day Agentic AI Challenge**. Over the next 30 days, I am completing 30 intensive agentic AI courses (from DeepLearning.AI, Coursera/IBM, Anthropic, and industry partners), extracting the core concepts using **Cursor**, and deploying a daily mini-project or writing a technical journal entry.

**My Goal:** To build a verifiable, public portfolio that goes beyond "knowing about agents" to actually engineering, debugging, and deploying production-ready multi-agent systems.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Tools
- **Core:** Python, OpenAI / Anthropic APIs, Open Source Models (Hugging Face)
- **Agent Frameworks:** LangChain, LangGraph, CrewAI, AutoGen/AG2, OpenAI Agents SDK, PydanticAI
- **RAG & Memory:** Weaviate, Neo4j (GraphRAG), Redis, LlamaIndex
- **Protocols:** MCP (Model Context Protocol), A2A, ACP
- **Observability:** LangSmith, Arize Phoenix, Weights & Biases
- **Deployment:** Streamlit, Gradio, Hugging Face Spaces, FastAPI
- **Dev Tools:** Cursor, Jupyter Notebooks
